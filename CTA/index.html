ABOUT US

Quality Truck Fleet

Featuring trucks by Volvo and Freightliner. Equipped with luxury condo high-rise cabins.

Including a 100 Foot Straight Line Back and 45 Degree Alley Dock simulation. Our range also permits training of parallel and serpentine maneuvers.

Student Housing and Dining Plans
